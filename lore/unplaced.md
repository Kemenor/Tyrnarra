- **Magitech, Magitrains, the Continental Rail Network, Train Pirates** — promoted from "Technology" placeholder to full canon 2026-05-17. Full canon in `cosmology.md` — *Technology — Magitech and Infrastructure*. Train Pirates entry in `factions.md` — *Bandit Categories*. **UHTC identity** remains the open thread (see `docs/open-threads.md`).
- **The Conductor's Station** — promoted 2026-05-17. Now canon as a rare pocket-dimension hazard of the Magitrain network specifically. Full entry in `cosmology.md` alongside Magitrains. Preferential network haunting (one vs both) is TBD.
- **Balaena, the Skywhale City** — placed 2026-05-17. Now in **Floteyn → Floating Isles of Shuun** (see `geography.md`). Rewritten to fit the drifting-island framing: Balaena reads as one of the hundreds of drifting islands of Shuun, with its skywhale nature hiding in plain sight among the actually-rooted drifters. The wooden footbridge connects to a neighbouring stable islet rather than a lakeshore. The Floteyn placement also explains why nobody questions a city that drifts subtly — *everything* drifts in Shuun.
- **The Red Empire** (and Iron Tide, Menagerie) — placed 2026-05-16. Located **off-continent**, across Hafra from Talan. Full entries now live in `factions.md` under a new *Off-Continent Powers* heading; geographic framing in `geography.md` under *Other Continents*. The bound thirteen reside on Talan, so the godless-mortal-supremacist Empire builds its anti-deity threat from a continent where the gods have no established sancta. Iron Tide raids on Talanese coasts are the active interface.
