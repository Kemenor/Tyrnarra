At a Glance

Bound God Churches
Thirteen. One per bound god. Each operates within whatever kingdom it sits in, subject to local law, but answers ultimately to its god's theology.
Other God Believers
Cults, churches, and traditions of non-bound gods, elven ancestral pantheons, demon lord cults, deep god worship, and others. Real divine power, active presence on Talan.
Authority
Local law first, theology second, but the theology is real, and so are the powers.
Reference
Each bound god's clergy structure lives on The Thirteen Bound Gods; the non-bound granters (Vice Demons, Virtue Devils, Layer-1 primordials, Layer-3 residents) live on Non-Bound Gods & Beings.

The Thirteen Bound Churches

Each of the thirteen bound gods has a church. Each church operates within whatever kingdom it sits in, subject to local law, but ultimately answers to their god's theology. See The Thirteen Bound Gods → for each god's clergy structure, to be expanded into individual pages here as content warrants.

✦

Other God Believers

An umbrella category for cults, churches, and traditions of non-bound gods. Elven ancestral pantheons, demon lord cults, deep god worship, and other pantheons all have real divine power and active churches on Talan. The thirteen are distinctive because they are present on the Material Plane and bound by the Gods' Law, not because they are the only gods. See Non-Bound Gods & Beings → for the named figures (Vice Demons, Virtue Devils, Layer-1 primordials, Layer-3 residents). To be developed.
