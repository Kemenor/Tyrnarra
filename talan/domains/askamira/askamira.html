At a Glance

Etymology
Basque aske (free) + mira (wonder, vision) → Askamira. Free wonder.
Position
Central Midarra — an island domain surrounded entirely by the freshwater inner sea
Terrain
Three completely distinct magical biomes coexisting on one island: ice in the north, desert/savanna in the centre, dense forest in the east. No single climate governs. Star Island lies nearby in Midarra.
Character
Magical island, three biomes, sea crossroads
