At a Glance

Etymology
Basque lautada (plain, flatland) → lauta → drifted to Lautara
Position
Northwest-central Talan, directly south of Vindul
Terrain
Sandy steppe and desert (dominant), grass steppe in the north (transition from Vindul), dramatic dark mountains along the western edge (Cloud Sea side), forested green highlands to the east, river system, coastal access to the southeast. Eastern cultural character.
Character
Trade routes, desert steppe, merchant cities
