The Thirteen Domains
One God, One Region, One Inseparable Bond
Talan is divided into thirteen domains — and the word is literal. Each is the bound territory of one of the Thirteen Bound Gods: the region's terrain, weather, peoples, and political character all bear the fingerprint of the god whose seat sits within it. A domain is not a country. Most contain many kingdoms, city-states, and sub-regions, often at odds with each other. What they share is the god whose presence shapes the air they breathe.
✦

What a Domain Is

The thirteen domains are the political and cosmological top-level of the continent. They were not drawn by treaty — they crystallised in the wake of the Week of Crimson Rain and the War of Seals, when the bound gods settled into the territories that had always answered to them. Each domain houses one god's city-state, one god's church-of-record, and as many secular kingdoms as history has given it. Some are mostly one kingdom (Zuzental's Thousand Kingdom dwarfs its neighbours); others are a confederation of dozens (Vindul, Nashavel); a few are barely-governed wilderness keyed to a god whose nature resists settlement (Ehizahar, Askamira).

The continent overview places the domains in their geography — three seas, two rail networks, one shared island at the centre. This page is the index into them.

The Thirteen

Vindul
Wind · Shuun's domain · Northern reaches
The wind-swept northern plateaus and the Shifting City of Haizava. A confederation of nomadic peoples bound less by border than by season.
Lautara
Commerce · Jianna's domain · Trade corridor
Merkavar's markets, the Kitsune Kingdom of Emarrea, and the Crossroads tri-domain nexus. The richest domain by gold, the most contested by everyone else.
Myrkono
Darkness · Araphel's domain · Shadow Steppes
Myrria, the City of Second Chances, and the bleak steppes that hide the Hollow of Ten Thousand Threads. Mercy lives here, but so do older things.
Floteyn
Water · Shuun's-twin domain · Floating Isles
The Floating Isles of Uravel, the Whale Shoals of Balatur Erui, and Balaena — the Skywhale City that draws breath against the upper sky.
Sumendar
Fire · Komo's domain · Volcanic spine
Eldara's forges, the Order of Steam's industrial heart, and Dragon's Reach — the city built from a fallen mothership. The continent's Magitech engine.
Lioaru
Time · Tani's domain · Desert and Lost Kingdom
The sand realm of Hareaveldi, the threefold sand-whale capital of Denbora, and the Lost Kingdom — the Blackened Lands where the old curse has not yet lifted.
Brauogi
Earth · Enki's domain · Stronghold country
Gotorlekua the Stronghold, Lurrath the Steadfast City, and the three-way of goblin, hobgoblin, and minotaur peoples that share the Earth domain.
Ezkudon
Knowledge · Vesuna's domain · Scholarly heartland
Thekkavar the City of Learning, the Calm Country of Lua Lasai, and the libraries that outlast every kingdom that has held them.
Egulon
Light · Iro's domain · Sun-blessed lowlands
Ljosarn the Everbright City, the radiant fields of Harro Distiratsue, and the warmest stretch of Calm Country on the continent.
Zuzental
Law · Forseti's domain · Codified kingdoms
Lograth the Judgment City, the dual-bloodline Thousand Kingdom, the Emerald Isles, and the Legea Empire — the densest concentration of formal law on Talan.
Nashavel
Chaos · Fisaya's domain · Unmapped country
Nahaskel the Unmapped City and the Kaosadaemi Principality. A domain where the maps disagree because the country itself disagrees with itself.
Ehizahar
Hunt · Hinka's domain · Wilderness and Fenurra
Veidrath the Hunting City, the Villtur tribal sprawl, and Fenurra — the volcanic Flame-Source where the Draconis tribe burns its rites.
Askamira
Freedom · Cronus's domain · The central island
Frae City at the heart of Midarra — Cronus's seat, the Council of Thirteen's venue, the most religiously diverse city on Talan, and the only landmass where ice, desert, and forest share a single shore.

Reading the Domains

Each domain page opens on the god whose name it carries and the city-state that god holds. From there, the page steps outward — sub-regions, kingdoms, settled territory, terrain. Where a sub-region has earned enough character to stand on its own, it has its own page linked from the parent. The promoted children currently live in Lautara (Emarrea, Crossroads), Myrkono (Myrria), Sumendar (Order of Steam, Dragon's Reach), Lioaru (Lost Kingdom), Zuzental (Thousand Kingdom, Emerald Isles, Legea Empire), and Ehizahar (Fenurra).

For the god-side reading — who each of the Thirteen is, rather than where they sit — start at The 13 Bound Gods.
