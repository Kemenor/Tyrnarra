At a Glance

Etymology
Basque nahas (chaos, disorder, to mix) → rearranged and drifted. The chaos in the name itself.
Position
East Talan, east of Ehizahar and Midarra, north of Zuzental and Egulon, coastal on Hafra
Terrain
Overwhelmingly dense old-growth forest: far more tree coverage than any other domain. Open grassland only in the south. A lake to the west, river in the south, mountains to the east. Wild magic woven into the trees themselves: paths don't stay fixed.
Character
Dense ancient forest, wild magic zones
