Egulon
Light Domain
Bound to Iro
Sunlight on open fields, not cold brilliance. The land where the most people simply live and prosper.
✦

At a Glance

Etymology
Basque eguzkia (sun) → compressed and drifted
Position
Southeast Talan, east of Ezkudon, south of Zuzental and Nashavel, coastal on Hafra
Terrain
Warm golden terrain throughout, open and relatively flat. Mountain ranges scattered through centre and west. Distinctive circular mountain ring with forest to the east. Large forested island off the southeast coast. The most settled and prosperous domain on the continent.
Character
Prosperous plains, divine light, southeast
