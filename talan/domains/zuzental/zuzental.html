At a Glance

Etymology
Basque zuzen (just, straight, correct) → drifted. "Straight land" with a built-in double meaning.
Position
East-central Talan, east of Midarra, north of Egulon, south of Nashavel and Ehizahar
Terrain
Green and open in the north, sandy steppe in the south, mountains along the western Midarra border. Dense forest in the centre-east. Forested islands off the eastern coast. Open, traversable land suited to roads, borders, and disputes about where borders fall.
Character
Open plains, codified civilization, empires

Sub-Regions

Emerald Isles
Modern English
Island kingdom of Zuzental. Three main forested islands off the east coast (Northern · Middle · Southern), plus the Bridgelands — a recently-acquired northeastern collection on the Hafra / Cloud-Sea boundary, connected by spanning Magitech bridges. The Bridgelands are the canonical landing point for cloudships from Sortalde and host the standing Sortalde embassies on Talan. Capital sits on Middle Isle. See dedicated page.

Thousand Kingdom
A thousand polities now consolidated into one
The dominant political sub-region of Zuzental. Two ruling bloodlines — elven long-planning and human short-planning — share authority under Forseti's law. Lograth sits within it and functions as both god-city and Kingdom capital; the two are effectively inseparable.
