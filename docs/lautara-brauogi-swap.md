# Lautara/Brauogi Region Swap

Tracking doc for the geography swap between **Lautara** (Commerce, Jianna) and **Brauogi** (Earth, Sarrum). Lautara moves to the south-central slot; Brauogi moves to the slot south of Vindul. The swap is geography-only: cosmology, gods, history, ancestries, Bolverk, and timeline are unaffected.

---

## Locked decisions

- **Lautara** moves to the south-central slot (currently Brauogi). New neighbours: Midarra (north, with Order of Steam's bay landing on Lautara-coast), Sumendar (west), Lioaru (south/southwest), Ezkudon (south/southeast), Egulon (east), Zuzental (northeast).
- **Brauogi** moves to the slot south of Vindul (currently Lautara). New neighbours: Vindul (north), Ehizahar (east), Midarra (east/southeast), Floteyn (southeast corner), Myrkono (south); Hafra coast to the west.
- **Emarrea = placement (a)**: western edge of the new Lautara slot, neighbouring **Hareaveldi** (Lioaru), **Tahu Tangata** (Sumendar), and the shrunken **Wildreach** (Lautara/Ezkudon border). Corner-touch with **Galdua Jendea** (Lioaru) so the Denbora sand-whale-city periodically drifts close. Downstream river-trade channel into the **River Duchies** as a secondary distribution backbone for sake/orchard exports. Eastern register (kotokoe / kitsune / Foxfire / Kyūbi-no-Den) is preserved as-is and *reinforces* Lautara's silk-road character; the cultural-character pages do not need rewriting, only neighbour-references.
- **Soul Tree stays geographically**, becomes a Brauogi sub-region. The island sits where it currently sits (offshore in Midarra, at what is currently Lautara's SE corner); new Brauogi occupies that slot, so the attribution flips without moving the island. Earth-domain affiliation is thematically cleaner: a great rooted tree through which souls pass is Sarrum-the-Steadfast iconography. Gives Brauogi a cosmologically-significant offshore site.
- **Twin Suns stays geographically**, becomes a Brauogi coastal sub-region. Same logic as Soul Tree: the coast stays put, the domain around it flips. Sub-region identity remains bound to Soul Tree's offshore presence.
- **Dreaming Cape stays geographically**, becomes a new-Lautara coastal sub-region. Sits in front of the OoS bay; new Lautara occupies the surrounding slot. Modern English name still fits the domain. Character can sharpen later: a dreamy mercantile reach where forge-smoke from OoS drifts in, sunsets read as omens, dock-side inns specialise in dream-blends.
- **Crossroads stays geographically**, becomes a Zuzental/Lautara/Egulon tri-state city. Original design intent restored.
- **Azkamour straddles Vindul and Brauogi**, **capital on the Vindul side** (revised 2026-05-25 from earlier Brauogi-side framing). Mostly golden Brauogi-character grain territory inland; the Vindul coastal stretch anchors the capital where storm-fishing meets grain-roads. Basque *azkar/muga* "Swift March" etymology still fits; the fast riders carry both ways.
- **Harro Distiratsue fully relocates into Egulon** (no longer a border territory). The Lautara/Egulon border shifts to put it entirely on the Egulon side. *Harro* (proud) + *distiratsu* (radiant) reads naturally as a pure Light-domain sub-region.
- **Wildreach elevated to a proper Ezkudon sub-region** (no longer a Lautara/Ezkudon border, no longer shrunken). The wild people live here; the area carries strong wild-magic / wild-fey character. Lautara loses Wildreach entirely; Emarrea's eastern neighbour is now Ezkudon directly via Wildreach.
- **Merkavar is an independent city-state-with-territory**, no longer inside Azkataria. The city, the lake, and the sacred peak **Helgafjall** together form Merkavar's domain; its authority extends to the ring of water and the sacred peak. **Azkataria** repurposed as the agricultural feeder belt south of Merkavar (the market-folk who supply the Market City).
- **Three new Lautara sub-regions named** (Basque/Icelandic deep-old stratum): **Itsasalda** (Basque *itsasoaldea* "seaside" → drift, northern coastal arm containing Rika Tikur and Dreaming Cape); **Atarialda** (Basque *atari* "threshold" + *aldea* → drift, eastern arm holding Crossroads at its NE corner); **Azkataria** repurposed as the agricultural feeder belt south of Merkavar.
- **Canon-additive landmarks** named: **Helgafjall** (Icelandic *helgr* "holy" + *fjall* "mountain", near-undrifted; sacred snow-capped peak rising from Merkavar's lake); **Yukihime-no-mori** (Kotokoe "Snow Princess Forest"; pink-blossomed orchard-and-wildflower lowlands at Emarrea's southern edge, source of Yukihime-shu plums); **Iratxobaso** (Basque *iratxo* "fey-creature" + *baso* "forest", near-undrifted; cluster of multicoloured trees at Wildreach's centre, source/focus of wild-magic).
- **Brauogi geographic features named** (all Basque deep-old stratum; Brauogi's naming culture is Basque-pure across the new canon): **Iturmen** (*iturri* + *mendi*, "source-mountain"; the central east-west spine, headwaters of most Brauogi rivers); **Iturburu** (*iturri* + *buru*, "source-head"; the great mountain-lake under Iturmen's tallest peak, practical-livelihood counterpart to Lautara's mythic-pilgrim Helgafjall); **Hirubaso** (*hiru* + *baso* from drifted *hiru-ibai-baso* "three-river-forest"; the sacred druid grove of Sarrum at the three-river confluence — the only routine Primotech site on the continent, where the rumoured Brauogi heart-stone is grown); **Eraztumen** (*eraztun* + *mendi*, "ring-mountain"; the ring of peaks around Lurrath that gives Gotorlekua its Stronghold name); **Mugamen** (*muga* + *mendi*, "border-mountain"; the southern range walling Brauogi off from Myrkono).
- **Hirubaso promoted to independent sub-region** (no longer a landmark inside Earth Realm). Governed by the druids' order itself; the Earth Realm becomes fully golden plains around it.
- **Iturmen + Iturburu unclaimed**: independent natural territory at the centre of the domain, belonging to no sub-region. Pass-towns on the spine answer to themselves; the source-lake and surrounding peaks are tended by druids attached to Hirubaso.
- **Baratalda** (new Brauogi sub-region): Basque *baratze* "garden, orchard" + *aldea* "region" → drift drops the medial *z* → **Baratalda** "the orchard-region." Eastern arm of Brauogi at the Ehizahar border; mixed golden plain and green orchard country. Frontier-trade kingdom where Brauogi farmers meet Ehizahar hunters: game-and-furs for wine, apple-brandy, and stone-fruit.
- **Twin Suns + Bikitsa shared border kingdom** (cross-domain governance, unique on Talan): Brauogi's **Twin Suns** (golden, food-rich coast, modern English name) and Myrkono's **Bikitsa** (Basque *biki-itzal* "twin-shadow", deep-old stratum) sit on opposite sides of the Brauogi/Myrkono coastal border, observe the same two-sun phenomenon, and are governed jointly under a single shared structure. The structure's specific form is canon-pending (liquid-democracy-style is the working hint). Soul Tree island remains Brauogi (Voroir Daua oldest charter).
- **Millhaven stays geographically**, becomes a Lautara settlement. The current `/talan/domains/brauogi/millhaven/` folder is a stub (no published lore yet); the swap reassigns it to Lautara as a settlement of the south-central slot. Fleshing-out deferred. Settlement folder path needs to move from `brauogi/` to `lautara/` when the HTML phase opens.
- **Merkavar stays an inland lake city** (within Azkataria's interior), not a Midarra coastal port. Silk-road great cities are inland (Samarkand / Bukhara register, not Tyre / Alexandria). The lake-and-river-hub framing supports multiple commercial centres across the domain (Merkavar luxury+caravan inland; Rika Tikur and Dreaming Cape coastal for bulk OoS-bay outflow), which is what "most flows through it" means as a *domain* rather than as a single city. The Market City byname reads as destination, not transit point.
- **Ancestries travel with the domains, not the geography.** South gets **Kitsune, Vishkanya, Halfling** (the Lautaran trade-folk register, fits Eastern silk-road domain-wide). North gets **Goblin, Hobgoblin, Minotaur** (Brauogi's Earth-domain anchor ancestries; Sarrum himself is a Minotaur, the cultural cousin line stays attached to Sarrum's domain).
- **Voroir Daua oldest charter on Soul Tree island** (new canon emerging from this swap). The cycle-warden order maintains a permanent presence directly on Soul Tree island; this charter is their *oldest* (predates the Lograth / Thousand-Kingdom / Myrkono-edge presence noted in current `factions.md`). The site's natural-soul-passage-to-Postlife role is exactly the cosmological function the order exists to watch, so charter primacy follows. Fold into `lore/factions.md` Voroir Daua entry and the `voroir-daua.html` Geography blocks.
- **Lautara/Floteyn/Myrkono tri-point port** (canon-pending placeholder): deleted, not relocated.
- **Order of Steam's Midarra outlet** (the bay through Rika Tikur / Dreaming Cape) now lands in Lautara-coast. Commerce-domain runs the port-of-egress for the industrial belt. This is the structural payoff.

### Domain character (ratified)

- **Lautara**: silk-road merchant feel preserved. Desert dominance dropped (Lioaru already carries the desert-nomad cultural register, and direct adjacency would make Lautara redundant on that axis). River + caravan + train commerce; "most flows through it" as the framing line. The new south-central position is the continent's commercial spine, with Order of Steam's bay outlet, Merkavar's lake-port, the River-Duchies downstream channel, and the Midarra southern shore all converging here. **Eastern cultural register applies domain-wide**, not only inside Emarrea: silk-road is itself Eastern-coded, and the "Eastern cultural character" line in current Lautara canon becomes explicit at every sub-region. Intensity varies by location (Emarrea full kotokoe; Merkavar / Azkataria silk-road merchant-Eastern; Dreaming Cape modern-Eastern mercantile-port; Rika Tikur drifted-Icelandic-but-mercantile reads as a hybrid Eastern trade-port; Crossroads stays modern-English-neutral as a functionally independent tri-state).
- **Brauogi**: rolling fertile lowlands, dense river networks, plentiful mountains the rivers spring from, earth tones throughout. The bread-named-twice domain sits where the north actually needs it, feeding Vindul / Ehizahar / Myrkono / Floteyn through nearby supply lines rather than long-haul Midarra shipping.
