Cleric Domains
Every canonical PF2e domain and its Tyrnarra source

Alphabetical master register. Granted by names the in-world being whose authority backs the domain grant. Source category identifies the class of granter — for filtering at a glance. A cleric channelling a domain answers theologically to the granter named here.

Reading the categories. Bound 13 are the Compact-bound gods of Talan, walking the Material Plane. Vice Demon are the seven seat-holders of Bolverk. Open covers the 8 domains still awaiting assignment (Generals of Corruption candidates, Betibizi, the Corrupted God, plus Dragon and Moon which have never been formally assigned).

No-sharing within a pantheon. Each pantheon — the Bound 13, the seven Vice Demons of Bolverk, the fourteen Virtue Devils to come — handles cleric domains under the same rule: within the pantheon, every domain is granted by exactly one member (the one whose aspects fit it most strongly within that pantheon). Cross-pantheon sharing is allowed — a domain can be granted by one Bound god AND one Vice Demon AND one Virtue Devil simultaneously, where each is the strongest fit within their own pantheon. The Granted by column lists all current grantors; a cleric reading the registrar sees every theological framing available for the domain they're channelling.

✦ Open / Canon-Pending

  • Bound Pass Locked46 of 61 PF2e domains assigned to the Thirteen. The bound-god domain pass is complete; within the Bound 13, every domain is granted by exactly one god — the deity whose aspects most directly align with it. Domains that fit two of the Thirteen conceptually are still held by one within their pantheon.
  • Vice Demon Pass LockedThe seven Vice Demons of Bolverk grant 25 PF2e domains between them — 3 to 5 per holder, with no sharing inside the pantheon. Each Vice Demon's full domain list: Drambur — Tyranny, Confidence, Zeal · Nirfel — Toil, Wealth, Secrecy, Travel · Lostar — Pain, Trickery, Passion, Dreams · Ofunda — Sorrow, Knowledge, Disorientation · Veislur — Indulgence, Decay, Plague, Family · Reidar — Destruction, Might, Vigil · Lethar — Abomination, Nightmares, Nothingness, Repose. Cross-pantheon sharing with the Bound 13 is allowed and frequent — 15 of these 25 are co-granted with a Bound god (Confidence shared with Sarrum, Trickery with Vesuna, Family with Jianna, and so on). A cleric channelling such a domain may answer theologically to either grantor; the registrar rows above list both.
  • Non-Bound Pending3 domains still genuinely pending. Naga, Swarm, Undeath — each carries an inline candidate (Generals of Corruption on Naga and Swarm, Betibizi on Undeath). Three further domains (Decay, Nightmares, Plague) have been claimed by Vice Demons (Veislur, Lethar, Veislur) but retain prior General-of-Corruption or Corrupted-God candidates that may eventually co-grant cross-pantheon when those characters are formalised.
  • Never Assigned2 domains have no prior canonical assignment on either side: Dragon and Moon. Plausible candidates exist (Zaharsuge for Dragon, Bikiargi for Moon), but neither has been formally taken up.
  • Lore-ImplicitTyrnarra-flavour names that aren't PF2e domains. Several names in the wider canon are theological flavour rather than PF2e cleric domains — Arcane Magic (Forseti), Divine Magic (Iro), Occult Magic (Enki), Primal Magic + Progress (Jianna), Free Will (Vesuna), Second Chances (Araphel) — and a cleric of these gods picks from their PF2e-canonical portfolios above. The flavour names are how worshippers describe their god's work in-world, not mechanical domains at character build. Full convention in lore/pf2e-notes.md.
