Cleric Domains
Every canonical PF2e domain and its Tyrnarra source

Alphabetical master register. Granted by names the in-world being whose authority backs the domain grant. Source category identifies the class of granter — for filtering at a glance. A cleric channelling a domain answers theologically to the granter named here.

Reading the categories. Bound 13 are the Compact-bound gods of Talan, walking the Material Plane — they claim 46 of the 61 PF2e cleric domains between them, with a strict no-sharing rule (each domain goes to the single god it fits most strongly). Open covers the remaining 15 domains. Of those, 13 await a dedicated non-bound assignment pass focused on the Vice Demons of Bolverk, the Virtue Devils, the Generals of Corruption, Betibizi, and the Corrupted God; their candidates are noted inline. The other two (Dragon, Moon) have plausible non-bound candidates but have never been formally assigned.

✦ Open / Canon-Pending

  • Bound Pass Locked46 of 61 PF2e domains assigned to the Thirteen. The bound-god domain pass is complete and follows the no-sharing rule: every domain that any of the Thirteen grants is granted by exactly one god — the deity whose aspects most directly align with it. Domains that fit two gods conceptually are still held by one; the other carries the flavour in lore (see Lore-Implicit below) but not the mechanical grant.
  • Non-Bound Pending13 domains awaiting a dedicated non-bound pass. Abomination, Decay, Destruction, Indulgence, Naga, Nightmares, Pain, Plague, Sorrow, Swarm, Toil, Tyranny, Undeath. Each row in the table above carries an inline candidate note pointing at the likely granter (Vice Demons of Bolverk on the vice-aligned domains, Virtue Devils on the corruption-of-virtue domains, Generals of Corruption on the underworld/plague/swarm domains, Betibizi on Undeath, the Corrupted God on Plague). These entities remain canonical beings — only the specific PF2e domain assignments are pending.
  • Never Assigned2 domains have no prior canonical assignment on either side: Dragon and Moon. Plausible candidates exist (Zaharsuge for Dragon, Bikiargi for Moon), but neither has been formally taken up.
  • Lore-ImplicitTyrnarra-flavour names that aren't PF2e domains. Several names in the wider canon are theological flavour rather than PF2e cleric domains — Arcane Magic (Forseti), Divine Magic (Iro), Occult Magic (Enki), Primal Magic + Progress (Jianna), Free Will (Vesuna), Second Chances (Araphel) — and a cleric of these gods picks from their PF2e-canonical portfolios above. The flavour names are how worshippers describe their god's work in-world, not mechanical domains at character build. Full convention in lore/pf2e-notes.md.
